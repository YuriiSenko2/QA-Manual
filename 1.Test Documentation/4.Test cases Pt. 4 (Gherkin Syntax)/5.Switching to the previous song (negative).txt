# Environment: Android 5 or higher

Feature. Tap combinations (Samsung Galaxy Buds Pro earbuds)

Example. Switching to the previous song.
 
Given. At least 30 % charged Samsung Galaxy Buds Pro earbuds
And at least 30 % charged smartphone
And the music player is installed on the smartphone
And two mp3 audio files are downloaded on the smartphone
And earbuds are connected to the smartphone via Bluetooth
And earbuds are held in the ears

When the second on the playlist song is played
And the sensor panel of one of the earbuds is quadruple-tapped

Then the second on the playlist song is still played
